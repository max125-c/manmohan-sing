Tribe To Manmohan Singh

Dr. Manmohan Singh was an esteemed Indian economist and politician, born on September 26, 1932, in Gah, Punjab (now in Pakistan). He is best known for his tenure as the Prime Minister of India from 2004 to 20142. Dr. Singh's academic journey was marked by brilliance, earning degrees from Panjab University, the University of Cambridge, and a D.Phil. from the University of Oxford. His career began in academia and international organizations before he entered Indian politics during the 1991 economic crisis as the Finance Minister2. His economic reforms during this period were pivotal in transforming India's economy. As Prime Minister, Dr. Singh led India through significant challenges and achievements, including the landmark India-US Civil Nuclear Agreement and various social welfare programs. He was widely respected for his integrity, humility, and dedication to public service1. Dr. Manmohan Singh passed away on December 26, 2024, leaving behind a legacy of leadership and economic transformation
